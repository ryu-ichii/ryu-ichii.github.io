[BIO]

Ryu Konrad is a writer based in Melbourne, Australia, currently completing a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Writing at RMIT University. Their work spans fiction, nonfiction, and electronic literature — exploring how digital space shapes the way stories are told and read.

Their electronic literature work, Working Title Psychosis, is a Twine-based narrative that experiments with non-linear structure and reader agency. Their nonfiction writing includes Swift Reflections and Process Documentation and Project Iteration, both of which trace the development of creative practice across digital platforms.

Ryu holds a Diploma of Professional Writing & Editing (2023) and a Certificate IV in Professional Writing & Editing (2022) from Swinburne University, completed prior to their current undergraduate study at RMIT.
